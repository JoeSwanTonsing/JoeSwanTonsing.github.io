Unlocking Potential, Igniting Change for Sustainable Development

Vision:
Local NGOs and development agencies from the North Eastern Region are empowered with data-driven insights and innovative M&E practices, ensuring sustainable development and transformative impact.

Mission:
To champion a culture of co-creation, collaboration, and partnership, enabling Frame Impacts to work alongside local NGOs and development agencies in Northeast India, crafting effective and inclusive models of change for a sustainable future.

What we do:

Unleashing Sustainable Impact through M&E Expertise
Frame Impacts Pvt. Ltd. is a trusted consultancy firm dedicated to empowering local organizations in Northeast India to achieve sustainable impact through robust monitoring and evaluation (M&E) expertise. We champion a collaborative approach, working closely with our clients to co-design and strategize programs, conduct rigorous social impact assessments, research, and generate data-driven insights for informed decision-making.
Our comprehensive services include:
  • Tailored M&E System Strengthening: We assist organizations in adopting and strengthening their M&E systems, ensuring program effectiveness, transparency, and accountability that aligns with their unique goals and objectives.
  • Co-creation of Impactful Solutions: We foster a collaborative approach to program design and evaluation, creating a learning environment that sparks innovative and impactful solutions tailored to specific needs.
  • Data-Driven Insights for Sustainable Development: We provide valid and sophisticated insights from program data, enabling informed decision-making that maximizes impact and drives sustainable development across Northeast India.
  • Transformative Communication and Storytelling: We transform complex reports into engaging narratives, helping organizations share their stories, garner support, and build positive social perceptions that amplify their impact.
  • Capacity Building for Organizational Transformation: We offer comprehensive capacity-building programs that equip teams with the skills and knowledge to conceptualize program design, implement social projects, develop outreach and communication strategies, form strong partnerships, and assess organizational impact for continuous improvement.
Choose Frame Impacts as your trusted partner and embark on a journey of sustainable impact in Northeast India. Together, we can create a brighter future for the region.
